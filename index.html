Onion prices go up after India slaps export duty

Although Bangladesh produces enough onion, a significant portion is imported each year to meet domestic demand>





Representational image

Onion prices at wholesale and retail markets in Bangladesh rose overnight today after India imposed a 40 percent duty on exports of the bulb. India slapped a fresh tax on onion exports on Saturday in a bid to retain stocks and thereby tame escalating domestic prices, according to a notification from the neighbouring nation's finance ministry.
